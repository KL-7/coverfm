Q: What is cover.fm?

A: Cover.fm is a small GAE-application, that grabs your favourite albums arts from last.fm and combines them all in one image.

Q: Should I manually update my TopArt?

A: No. This service updates all TopArts periodically. Now update occures every tree hours, but in production version TopArts will be updated once per day.

Q: Why can't I use it right now?

A: This application is not ready for public use right now (it's very unstable, has lots of bugs, etc.), but I hope it'll be ready one day =) If you really want to get your own TopArt I'll be happy to register it for you and get any reports and suggestions from you in return. Just send me a mail (kl7.666 at gmail.com) or left a shout in my last.fm profile.
